Leaders in Pharmaceutical Excellence
Radicon laboratories limited was Established in the year 2007. It has carved a niche for itself in the pharmaceutical sector globally. Our manifesto of working is to procure pure quality of drugs. We are a reputed and esteemed manufacturer, exporter, trader and marketing agent with a range of hygienic and efficacious life-saving medicines. Radicon’s product portfolio includes prescription medicines and vaccines. The company is the market leader in the most of the therapeutic categories in which it operates. Radicon is best in class field force, backed by a nation-wide network of stockiest to ensure that the company’s products are readily available across the nation. Radicon have a large scale manufacturing unit in India, located at greater Noida industrial area. The state of art plant at greater Noida is technically designed according to the WHO-GMP guidelines which give us an added advantage of procuring better and safe drugs.
Ensuring Availability, Enhancing Lives
Being one the leader brings responsibility towards the community in which we operate. At radicon we have a CSR program that work towards fulfilling basic healthcare, education and other development needs of the underserved population. With this dedication and commitment, we believe that the world will be better, healthier, and happier place to live. We follow revised WHO GMP guidelines that have been certified & accredited for Good Manufacturing Practices (GMP). We have Export House Status,accorded by Ministry of Commerce, Govt. of India. We have separate division for Human as well as Veterinary segments. We have achieved new heights in manufacturing of diverse range of products such as capsules, tablets and ointments in different therapeutic segments and dietary supplements.
Delivering Quality Medicines for a Healthier World
The unit has been established with the aim to provide high quality and cost effective medicines to suffering masses for domestic, semi-regulated markets and rest of the world. All the key operations have been assigned to highly qualified full time professionals. The company is well-respected in the pharmaceutical market for its reliable products, services and for quality of personnel. Radicon group have well equipped laboratories and world class R & D facilities with a pool of experienced professionals, scientists and medical professionals to produce the most advance range of safe, effective and affordable medicines.
