FAQs

What is Radicon Laboratories's role in the pharmaceutical sector?
Radicon Laboratories is a reputed pharmaceutical company that operates globally, manufacturing and exporting a wide range of high-quality and affordable drugs. The company is known for its leadership in various therapeutic categories and its commitment to delivering life-saving medicines.
How does Radicon Laboratories ensure the quality and safety of its products?
Radicon Laboratories follows the revised WHO-GMP guidelines and adheres to Good Manufacturing Practices (GMP) to ensure the highest standards of quality. The company's manufacturing facilities are designed according to these guidelines, and all key operations are overseen by highly qualified professionals.
What certifications has Radicon Laboratories obtained?
Radicon Laboratories follows revised WHO GMP guidelines and has been certified and accredited for Good Manufacturing Practices (GMP). The company has also been accorded Export House Status by the Ministry of Commerce, Government of India.
Where is Radicon Laboratories's manufacturing unit located?
Radicon Laboratories has a large-scale manufacturing unit located in the greater Noida industrial area of India. The state-of-the-art plant is designed in accordance with the WHO-GMP guidelines, ensuring the production of better and safe drugs.
What is the market reach of Radicon Laboratories Laboratories's products?
Radicon Laboratories operates in both domestic and global markets. More than 75% of its total production is exported to countries such as Afghanistan, Iraq, CIS countries, African countries, Uzbekistan, Tajikistan, Ghana, Nigeria, Myanmar, Russia, and Vietnam.
