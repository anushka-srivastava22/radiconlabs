Unleashing the Power of Discovery
RADICON lays strong emphasis on research towards development of processes and products that are patent non-infringing – a route that can propel the company towards attaining a formidable position in the markets. Along with quality and innovation, research and development forms the core in the overall strategy for global success. RADICON has a vibrant product development centre that caters to both in-house and collaborative manufacturers’ needs. To meet its technical support, the R & D block houses the latest hi-tech equipment of reputed European and American brands in the industry.
Shaping the Future of Medicine
The R & Ds main focus is on generic drugs formulations and new drug delivery systems. The key objectives of RADICON’S research and development include faster onset to market new products and process development and technology transfer that is simple robust and consistent. Product development activity includes novel formulations such as mouth dissolving tablets, controlled release tablets, effervescent tablets, bi-layered tablets, enteric coated pellets and special capsule formulations such as pellet and powder in capsule and tablet and pellet in capsule. On the other end of the spectrum is packaging development that ensures the packaging is aesthetically appealing, user friendly and at the same time product stability is maintained throughout the shelf life.Formulation Research and Development in RADICON is accomplished by experienced scientists, technicians and chemists. The team specializes in creating alternate and patentable processes for generic formulations.
Empowering Healthcare Solutions
It is equipped to conduct the following processes for formulation development:
  • Antibiotics
  • Product and process optimization
  • Analytical method development
  • Analytical method validation
    • Evaluation and techno-marketing studies
  • Technology transfer
Innovating for Better Patient Experience
Our scientists, with pre-formulation insight and proficient knowledge in pharmaceutical ingredients, are equipped to create the best combination of materials and manufacturing methods. The R&D facility is geared toward development of technologically challenging products. Over the years, the Radicon's R&D innovation team has created several distinctive technology-based products which improve patient convenience and compliance. Some examples of such ‘Value-added generics’ include dispersible tablets, orally dissolving films, candy lozenges, multi-layered tablets, etc. We strive to develop cost-effective, high-quality formulations to make the life of the patient better.
